# Dongpo Pork Recipe Project

The main function of this project is to create a pure JavaScript application that presents an interactive recipe for Dongpo Pork. The application includes several key features:

## Key Features

1. **Responsive Design**: The project uses HTML and CSS to ensure that the page is responsive and looks good on various screen sizes.

   **主要功能1. 响应式设计**：该项目使用HTML和CSS确保页面在各种屏幕尺寸下都能良好显示。

2. **Interactive Elements**: The project includes interactive elements such as buttons and modals to enhance user experience.

   **主要功能2. 交互元素**：该项目包括按钮和模态框等交互元素，以增强用户体验。

3. **Dynamic Content Loading**: JavaScript is used to dynamically load and manipulate content on the webpage, providing a more engaging experience for users.

   **主要功能3. 动态内容加载**：该项目使用JavaScript动态加载和操作网页内容，为用户提供更吸引人的体验。

4. **Local Development Server**: A local development server using Browser Sync to provide live reloading and a streamlined development workflow.

   **主要功能4. 本地开发服务器**：该项目使用Browser Sync的本地开发服务器，提供实时重新加载功能和简化的开发工作流程。

Overall, this project aims to combine modern web development techniques with a user-friendly interface to showcase the recipe for Dongpo Pork in an engaging and interactive manner.

总体而言，该项目旨在结合现代Web开发技术与用户友好的界面，以一种吸引人且互动的方式展示东坡肉食谱。

## 回锅肉 Recipe

### Ingredients
- 500g pork belly
- 1 green pepper
- 1 red pepper
- 2 tablespoons of oil
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of fermented black beans
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 piece of ginger
- 3 tablespoons of water

### Preparation Steps
1. Boil the pork belly for 20 minutes, then let it cool and slice it thinly.
2. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
3. Add the pork belly slices and stir-fry until they start to brown.
4. Add the soy sauce, fermented black beans, and water, and mix well.
5. Add the green and red peppers and stir-fry for another 5 minutes.
6. Serve hot.

### Notes
- Make sure to slice the pork belly as thin as possible for the best texture.
- Adjust the amount of soy sauce and black beans according to your taste preference.

## 东坡肉 Recipe

### Ingredients
- 500g pork belly
- 1 green onion
- 2 tablespoons of oil
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1/4 cup of Chinese rice wine
- 4 tablespoons of sugar
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 piece of ginger
- 1 star anise
- 3 tablespoons of water

### Preparation Steps
1. Blanch the pork belly in boiling water for 5 minutes, then remove and let it cool. Cut into 2-inch squares.
2. Heat oil in a pot and add the sugar, stirring until it melts and turns golden brown.
3. Add the pork belly pieces to the pot and brown them on all sides.
4. Add the soy sauce, Chinese rice wine, and water, and bring to a boil.
5. Add the garlic, ginger, green onion, and star anise. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour until the meat is tender and the sauce is thick.
6. Serve hot, garnished with additional green onion if desired.

### Notes
- For an authentic flavor, use Chinese rice wine instead of other cooking wines.
- Adjust the amount of sugar for a sweeter or less sweet dish.
